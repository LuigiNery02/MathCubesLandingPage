Test your strategic and matching skills as you arrange the cubes on the board

Combine, Grow, and Survive

Place and combine cubes with the same value to create a new cube equal to their total sum. Every successful merge increases your score, but also makes the board more crowded. You’ll need to plan ahead and think carefully, because there’s a death zone on the board. If any cube reaches that area, the game is over.

Reach the Exact Value

In Challenge Mode, a random target value is generated at the start of the round. Your goal is to combine cubes until you create a cube with exactly the same value as the target.

Play, Earn, Customize

As you play, you earn coins based on your score. These coins can be used to unlock environment customizations and background music, letting you personalize the look and feel of the game.

Watch Ads to Get Special Cubes!

X Cube

The X Cube can combine with any cube, regardless of its value. When it merges, it acts as a x2 multiplier, doubling the value of the cube it combines with.

Explosive Cube

The Explosive Cube detonates on contact with any cube, destroying nearby cubes and clearing space on the board. All destroyed cubes add their values directly to your score.
